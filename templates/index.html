About the System

This system uses facial encodings for accurate identification. It is designed for simplicity and efficiency, making it suitable for academic research and industrial deployment. The backend is powered by Flask and the face_recognition library based on dlib's deep learning models, achieving near state‑of-the‑art accuracy on benchmark datasets. All recognition happens locally on your machine — no data is sent to external servers.
